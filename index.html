Privacy Policy — محاسبة (Muhasaba)

Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the محاسبة (Muhasaba) mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address, and a unique account identifier. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Your activity content. The deeds, dhikr/azkar counts, notes, and progress history you record in the App.
  • Group information. If you use the Groups feature, your name and your recorded progress are visible to the supervisor/teacher and members of groups you join.

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising, and the App contains no ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features: tracking your deeds and dhikr, showing your history and analytics.
  • To sync your data across your devices when you are signed in.
  • To enable the Groups feature (showing member names and shared progress to the relevant group).

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Processing

Your data is stored on your device and, when you sign in, synced to our backend provider Supabase, which stores data on secure cloud infrastructure on our behalf. Data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS.

4. Offline Use

The App works offline. If you do not sign in, your data remains on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with the service providers required to run the App (Google for sign-in, Supabase for storage/sync). We do not share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below; we will delete your synced data from our backend upon verified request. Uninstalling the App removes locally stored data from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
