Data Deletion — محاسبة (Muhasaba)

App: محاسبة (Muhasaba) · Developer: Abdelrahman Hosny · Last updated: 20 June 2026

This page explains how to delete your data from the محاسبة (Muhasaba) app, either in part or entirely, with or without deleting your account.

1. Delete specific data inside the app

You can delete your own content directly within the app at any time, without deleting your account:

  • Open the app and go to the deed, dhikr, or log you want to remove.
  • Delete it from there — the item and its history are removed from your device, and the deletion is synced to our servers if you are signed in.

2. Delete your account and all associated data

To request deletion of your entire account and all data synced to our servers, contact us:

  • Email: 7osny12@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line "Delete my account" and send from the email address associated with your account.

We will verify the request and delete your data from our backend.

What is deleted and what is kept

  • Deleted: your account profile (name, email, user identifier), and all your deeds, dhikr counts, notes, logs, history, and group memberships stored on our servers.
  • Local data: uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • Retention: we delete your synced data from our backend upon a verified request. Backups, if any, are purged within 90 days. We do not retain personal data beyond what is needed to operate the app.
